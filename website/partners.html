Partners We're Seeking

Universities & Research Institutions

What's in it for you: First-mover research opportunity in a field with zero peer-reviewed literature. Student thesis opportunities. Co-authorship on publications. Funded work packages in EU grant applications.

What we need: Faculty advisor, testing equipment access, letter of support for grants, 1–2 students for 3–6 month research projects.

Express interest →

Municipalities & Local Government

What's in it for you: A €25,000 affordable housing solution for your constituents. EU funding alignment (NEB, Horizon Europe, LIFE). Circular economy showcase. Community engagement through build workshops.

What we need: Land access (200 m² minimum, flat), simplified permitting guidance, community introductions, letter of support for grants.

Express interest →

NGOs & Housing Organisations

What's in it for you: A proven, buildable, documented system for your housing programmes. Open-source — no licensing fees. Scalable from one home to hundreds. Local materials — no import dependency.

What we need: Field testing in your region, community connections, translation of build manual, advocacy in your network.

Express interest →

Structural Engineers

What's in it for you: First licensed engineer to sign off on pallet-based construction. Unique CV entry. Co-authorship on peer-reviewed papers. €2,000–€6,000 fee for initial review.

What we need: Eurocode calculation review (EC 1, 5, 8), panel assembly analysis, foundation design, signed documentation for permits, 2–3 hours/month advisory during build.

Apply →

Builders & Contractors

What's in it for you: New revenue stream — offer pallet-based construction as a low-cost alternative. Marketing differentiation. Low material costs. Speed — 10-day build = more projects per year.

What we need: Build the prototype with us (volunteer or reduced fee), feedback on build manual, real-world cost data, optional: offer Hestia builds as a service.

Express interest →

Material Suppliers

What's in it for you: Your materials specified in open-source build manual used globally. "Our insulation is in the €25,000 open-source house." Bulk orders as project scales. Sustainability alignment.

What we need: Technical data sheets, project pricing or material donations, technical support during build, optional: co-branded product bundles.

Express interest →
