Project Timeline

From founding to prototype build to EU grant applications — our roadmap through 2028.

2026 — Foundation & Build

May 2026

Project Founded

Hestia Foundation established in Lagos, Portugal. Strategy documents, bylaws, and initial website drafted. 40+ documents published on GitHub.

June 2026

Legal Registration & Team Assembly

Register Portuguese non-profit (Associação). Identify 3 founding members. Get Portuguese NIFs. Open bank account. Recruit build team volunteers.

July 2026

Material Procurement

Order 80–100 EPAL pallets, I-joists, OSB sheathing, insulation, roofing, electrics, plumbing, sensors. Arrange delivery to site. Finalise build team (4–6 people).

August–September 2026

Shell A Prototype Build

10 active days of construction in Algarve. Foundation, floor, walls, roof, electrics, plumbing, insulation, interior. Install 20+ sensors. Begin 12-month monitoring.

October 2026

Documentation & NEB Boost

Publish build manual v1.0 with as-built photos, costs, and lessons learned. Submit NEB Boost application (€30K). First quarterly monitoring report.

November–December 2026

HORIZON-NEB Proposal

Submit HORIZON-NEB-2026-01-REGEN-01 proposal (€4M) with full consortium (UAlg, LNEC, Lagos Municipality, structural engineer, + 2 partners). Apply for NEB Facility, LIFE, Portugal 2030 as appropriate.

2027 — Testing, Grants & Scale

Q1 2027

NEB Boost Result & Continued Monitoring

NEB Boost result (€30K). Second quarterly monitoring report. Winter performance data from Algarve prototype. Apply for additional small grants.

Q2 2027

HORIZON-NEB Result

€4M HORIZON-NEB evaluation result (Portugal success rate: 39.3%). If funded: begin consortium mobilisation, hire staff, plan 3–5 pilot homes. If not: survival mode — crowdfunding, consulting, smaller grants.

Q3 2027

Spring Performance & Expansion Planning

Third quarterly monitoring report. Storm season performance data. If HORIZON-NEB funded: begin pilot home designs, site selection, partner agreements. If not: focus on grant diversification and volunteer network growth.

Q4 2027

Full-Year Analysis

Fourth quarterly monitoring report. Complete 12-month sensor dataset. Publish full thermal and structural performance analysis. Begin peer-reviewed paper preparation.

2028 — Scale or Consolidate

Q1–Q2 2028

Pilot Builds (If Funded)

Construct 3–5 pilot homes across Portugal (Algarve + interior regions). Each home tests a climate adaptation. Student research continues. Builder training programme begins.

Q3–Q4 2028

Publish & Advocate

Submit peer-reviewed paper on pallet construction performance. Present at EU Housing Forum or New European Bauhaus Festival. Publish build manual v2.0 with all climate adaptations. Apply for Mécénio / Patronage status (tax-deductible donations).

Ongoing

  • Monthly: Sensor data review, site visit, maintenance log
  • Quarterly: Monitoring report published on GitHub + website
  • Annually: Full structural inspection, LCA update, financial audit, General Assembly
