The Problem

Since 2013, EU house prices have risen 60%. Portugal's have risen 94% — 4th highest in Europe. Social housing is only 6–7% of EU stock. Meanwhile, 2.5 billion pallets circulate in the EU, many discarded.

The Solution

The Ribbed Composite Panel (RCP) uses standard EPAL Euro pallets as the primary structural element, reinforced with I-joists and sheathed with OSB. A complete 37.5 m² home costs €25,000 and can be built by non-specialists in 10 days. All plans are CC BY-SA 4.0.

The Prototype

Shell A will be built in Algarve, Portugal, Q3 2026. Monitored with 20+ sensors for 12 months. Independently tested by LNEC. Student research with University of Algarve. All data published openly.

EU Alignment

Aligned with New European Bauhaus, European Green Deal, Horizon Europe, and LIFE Programme. Targeting €4M HORIZON-NEB-2026-01-REGEN-01 grant (deadline 01/12/2026). Portugal's Horizon Europe success rate: 39.3% (2.5× EU average).

Partners

University of Algarve (civil engineering), LNEC (structural testing), Lagos Municipality (land, permits). Seeking: structural engineer, additional universities, material suppliers, funders.
