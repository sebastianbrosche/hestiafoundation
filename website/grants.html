Grants & Funding

We're applying for EU and national grants to fund the prototype, scale the system, and publish open-source knowledge for anyone to use.

Our Funding Strategy

Hestia Foundation is a non-profit with no revenue model. We rely on grants, donations, and volunteer labour. Our strategy is three-tier:

  • Seed funding (€10–50K) — Prototype build, legal registration, initial documentation
  • Medium grants (€30K–500K) — NEB Boost, NEB Facility, Portugal 2030, LIFE Programme
  • Major grants (€2–8M) — Horizon Europe, HORIZON-NEB, Urban Innovative Actions

Why Hestia Wins Grants

🇪🇺

EU Priority Alignment

Housing affordability is a top EU priority. The Commission has mobilised €43 billion (2021–2027) and targets €375 billion by 2029. Hestia directly addresses this crisis.

📈

Portugal's Success Rate

Portugal has a 39.3% Horizon Europe success rate — 2.5× the EU average of 15.9%. As a Widening country, Portugal has access to dedicated funding instruments.

🔓

Open-Source Multiplier

Every euro invested creates public knowledge, not private IP. A €4M grant funds a system that thousands of families can use for €25,000 each — a 1:1000 leverage ratio.

🤝

Strong Partners

University of Algarve (civil engineering, student research), LNEC (structural testing), Lagos Municipality (land, permits, community).

EU Policy Context

  • New European Bauhaus: €120M/year (2025–2027). Hestia is beautiful (honest design), sustainable (circular, low-carbon), and together (community builds).
  • European Green Deal: Construction is a priority sector. Hestia eliminates concrete (8% of global CO₂) and uses waste-stream materials.
  • EU Affordable Housing Initiative: Part of the European Pillar of Social Rights. Target: lift 15 million people out of poverty by 2030. Housing is the lever.
  • Circular Economy Action Plan: 2.5 billion pallets circulate in the EU annually. Reusing 80–100 per home is circular construction in practice.

How to Support Us

If you're an individual

Donations will be accepted once our non-profit registration and bank account are complete. Check back here for updates.

If you're a foundation or philanthropist

We need €10,000–€50,000 in seed funding for the prototype build and legal registration. This is high-leverage: it enables us to apply for €4M in EU grants. Contact us.

If you're a company

Material suppliers, builders merchants, and logistics companies: we need project pricing or donations of pallets, I-joists, OSB, insulation, and roofing materials. In exchange, you'll be named in all documentation and case studies. Partner with us.

If you're a grant professional

We are seeking a Horizon Europe grant writer (€3,000–€6,000 fee for HORIZON-NEB proposal). View the brief on GitHub.
