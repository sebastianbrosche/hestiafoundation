# Hestia Foundation — One-Page Pitch

## The Problem

- **€43 billion** mobilised by the EU for housing — yet prices rose 60% since 2013
- **Only 6–7%** of EU housing stock is social housing
- **20%** of homes remain unoccupied while families cannot afford to buy
- **2 million** homes needed per year — conventional construction cannot scale
- **€150,000+** for a conventional family home — requires 30-year mortgage

---

## How It Works

**Materials:** CE-marked timber I-joists + EPAL Euro pallets + 12mm OSB sheathing

**Assembly:**
1. Earth screw foundation (no concrete)
2. Fabricate wall panels on-site (1.2m × 3.6m, 45kg each)
3. Erect panels, connect with L-brackets
4. Install roof I-joists and membrane
5. Add insulation, windows, off-grid systems
6. Interior finishes — walnut, bronze, engineered oak

**Result:** A weathertight, highly insulated, off-grid family home built by non-specialists in under two weeks.
