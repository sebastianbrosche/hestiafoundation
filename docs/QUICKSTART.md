## Your First Contribution

1. **Read** the relevant docs above
2. **Introduce yourself** via email or GitHub issue
3. **Pick a task** from `docs/HESTIA_ACTION_LIST.md` or open issues
4. **Ask questions** — we'd rather answer than have you guess wrong
5. **Document everything** — If you learn something, write it down

## Values and Culture

- **No corporate speak.** We build houses. We say what we mean.
- **Open by default.** If it's not personal or confidential, it's public.
- **Safety first.** Never rush at the expense of safety.
- **Document failures.** They teach more than successes.
- **Help each other.** We're a small team. Ask for help. Offer help.

---

## Safety

If you're joining the build:
- Read the safety briefing in `docs/build/WORKSHOP_FACILITATOR_GUIDE.md`
- Bring steel-toe boots, work clothes, sun protection
- Hydration is non-negotiable in Algarve heat
- No one works alone. Always in pairs.
- If you're tired, you rest. No heroics.
