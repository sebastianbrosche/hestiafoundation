## The EU Housing Crisis — Official Context

### European Commission Data (Verified)
- **House prices:** Up 60% across EU since 2013
- **Rents:** Up 20%
- **Social housing:** Only 6–7% of EU housing stock (among lowest globally)
- **Unoccupied housing:** 20% of stock sits empty
- **Short-term rentals:** Surged 93% (2015–2019)
- **Funding mobilised:** €43 billion (2021–2027) + €10 billion additional (2026–2027)
- **Target:** €375 billion mobilised by 2029

### Portugal-Specific
- **House prices:** Up 94% since 2013 (4th highest in EU)
- **Social housing:** ~2% of stock (one of the lowest in EU)
- **Tourism pressure:** Algarve heavily affected by short-term rental demand
- **Rural depopulation:** Interior regions losing population, housing stock deteriorating

Source: European Commission, DG ECFIN, Housing Market Statistical Briefing, 2025

## EU Strategies Hestia Aligns With

### 1. New European Bauhaus (NEB)
**What it is:** An EU initiative to make sustainability beautiful, affordable, and inclusive. €120 million/year through Horizon Europe (2025–2027).

**How Hestia fits:**
- **Beautiful:** Simple, honest design. Natural materials. No pretension.
- **Sustainable:** Circular economy (waste pallets), no concrete, low carbon.
- **Together:** Community build workshops. Open-source knowledge. Anyone can build.
- **Affordable:** €25,000 vs. €150,000+. Directly addresses housing affordability.

**Relevant calls:**
- HORIZON-NEB-2026-01-REGEN-01 (€4M/project, deadline 01/12/2026)
- NEB Boost (€30K, rural communities)
- NEB Facility (€50K–5M)

### 2. European Green Deal
**What it is:** EU plan to be climate-neutral by 2050. Construction is a priority sector.

**How Hestia fits:**
- **No concrete:** Cement industry = 8% of global CO₂. We eliminate it.
- **No structural steel:** Mining and smelting eliminated.
- **Circular materials:** 2.5 billion pallets circulate in EU annually. We reuse them.
- **Low embodied carbon:** Target <200 kg CO₂e/m² vs. 300–500 conventional.
- **Passive performance:** Mineral wool insulation reduces heating/cooling demand.

**Relevant policies:**
- Circular Economy Action Plan (construction and buildings)
- Renovation Wave Strategy
- Energy Performance of Buildings Directive (EPBD)

### 3. EU Affordable Housing Initiative
**What it is:** Part of the European Pillar of Social Rights. Target: lift 15 million people out of poverty by 2030. Housing is a key lever.

**How Hestia fits:**
- **Cost:** €25,000 is genuinely affordable for low-income families.
- **Self-build:** Eliminates developer profit margin (typically 20–30% of cost).
- **Scalable:** If 10,000 families build = €250M in housing value, €0 in public subsidy.
- **Rural focus:** Works in areas where conventional construction is too expensive.

### 4. Horizon Europe (Cluster 4: Digital, Industry and Space)
**What it is:** EU research and innovation programme. Cluster 4 includes construction, materials, and digitalisation.

**How Hestia fits:**
- **Innovation:** No peer-reviewed literature on pallet construction. We're creating the field.
- **Digital:** Open-source CAD, sensor networks, data dashboards, GitHub repository.
- **Industry:** Potential for EU-wide pallet construction industry.
- **Widening:** Portugal qualifies for Widening participation (39.3% success rate).

**Relevant calls:**
- HORIZON-CL4-2026 (construction and built environment)
- HORIZON-NEB-2026 (New European Bauhaus)

### 5. LIFE Programme
**What it is:** EU funding for environment and climate action. €3.5 billion (2021–2027).

**How Hestia fits:**
- **Circular economy:** Waste stream (pallets) → housing resource.
- **Climate mitigation:** Low embodied carbon, passive performance.
- **Adaptation:** Lightweight, movable structures for flood-prone or vulnerable areas.

### 6. Cohesion Policy / Portugal 2030
**What it is:** EU structural funds for regional development. Portugal receives significant allocations.

**How Hestia fits:**
- **Regional development:** Algarve is a priority region (tourism-dependent, housing pressure).
- **Social inclusion:** Affordable housing for low-income families.
- **Innovation:** Novel construction system with potential for regional industry.

## National Policy Alignment (Portugal)

### Portugal 2030 Strategy
- **Innovation:** Hestia is a novel construction technology.
- **Sustainability:** Aligns with Portugal's carbon neutrality target (2050).
- **Social cohesion:** Addresses housing affordability in Algarve and interior regions.

### Plano de Recuperação e Resiliência (PRR)
- **Housing component:** €1.5 billion for housing and urban rehabilitation.
- **Digitalisation:** Open-source digital tools for construction.
- **Green transition:** Low-carbon construction systems.

### Programa de Arrendamento Acessível (PAA)
- Government programme for affordable rental housing.
- Hestia could supply low-cost housing stock for PAA.

## Talking Points for Policymakers

### For EU Officials
> "Hestia is not asking for subsidies. We're asking for the EU to invest in open-source knowledge that can produce millions of homes without ongoing public cost. A €4M grant creates a system that any family can use for €25,000. That's €0.16 per home in public investment if 25,000 families build."

### For Portuguese Officials
> "Portugal has the 4th highest house price increase in the EU. We also have the highest Horizon Europe success rate (39.3%). Hestia combines Portugal's research strength with its housing crisis. We can be the EU's open-source housing laboratory."

### For Municipal Officials
> "A Hestia home costs less than a parking space to build. It requires no concrete, no heavy machinery, and minimal permitting. It's the fastest way to add affordable housing stock to your municipality."

### For MEPs
> "The EU has mobilised €43 billion for housing. Most of it goes to conventional construction. What if 1% went to open-source systems? That €430M could fund 430 projects like Hestia, each creating a buildable system for thousands of families."
