## Verified Intelligence (from Appendix N)

### EU Housing Context
- House prices in EU up 60% since 2013
- Rents up ~20% in same period
- Only 6–7% of EU housing stock is social housing
- 20% of homes remain unoccupied
- Short-term rentals surged 93% (2018–2024)
- Europe needs 2M+ homes per year
- €43B mobilised by EU (2021–2027) + €10B additional (2026–2027)
- €375B targeted by partner financial institutions by 2029
