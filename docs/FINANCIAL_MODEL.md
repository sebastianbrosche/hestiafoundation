## Break-Even Analysis

**Minimum viable funding for 2026–2027:**
- €24,000 to cover Year 1 (prototype + legal + grant writing)
- €20,000 for Year 2 Q1–Q2 (survival mode)
- **Total: €44,000**

**Sources to cover this:**
- NEB Boost: €30,000 (most achievable)
- NEB Facility: €50,000 (backup)
- Crowdfunding: €10,000–20,000 (parallel track)
- Donations: €5,000–10,000
- Founder / friends & family: €5,000–10,000

**Conclusion:** If we secure NEB Boost (€30K) + one smaller source, we survive to Q2 2027. If we also get HORIZON-NEB, we scale significantly.
