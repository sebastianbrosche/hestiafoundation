## Why Portugal Gives Hestia an Edge

| Advantage | What It Means for Hestia |
|-----------|--------------------------|
| **Widening Country Status** | Access to dedicated funding instruments (Twinning, Teaming, ERA Chairs) not available to all EU members |
| **39.3% Horizon Europe Success Rate** | Nearly 2.5x the EU average (15.9%). Portuguese-led proposals have proven competitive advantage. |
| **€490M Secured (2021–2026)** | Portugal punches above its weight. Strong track record = credibility by association. |
| **€179.6M Widening Funding** | Dedicated budget lines for countries with lower R&D investment. Hestia qualifies. |
| **ANI Free Advisory** | National Contact Point provides FREE pre-submission review and partner search. |
| **Algarve = Rural + Coastal** | Perfect fit for NEB rural communities call (Lagos < 20,000 residents). |

## The Portugal Playbook

### Step 1: Contact ANI (Week 1)
- **What:** Free advisory session on Horizon Europe Pillar II / NEB calls
- **How:** Email or contact form via ani.pt
- **Ask:** "We are a Portuguese non-profit (in formation) building open-source affordable housing. Which Horizon Europe and NEB calls are the best fit for our project?"
- **Outcome:** Validated call list + potential partner introductions

### Step 2: Secure University Partner (Week 1–2)
- **Target:** UAlg — Elisa da Silva (esilva@ualg.pt)
- **Ask:** Letter of intent for structural engineering support + student involvement
- **Value-add:** University credentials boost proposal credibility; students = labour + research data

### Step 3: Secure LNEC Partnership (Week 2–3)
- **Target:** Marta Rodrigues (mfrodrigues@lnec.pt)
- **Ask:** Independent structural testing of pallet wall panels; certification support
- **Value-add:** Third-party validation is gold for grant proposals and building permits

### Step 4: Hire Grant Professional (Week 3–4)
- **Budget:** €3,000–€8,000 for proposal writing
- **Profile:** Horizon Europe experience, NEB knowledge, Portuguese + English
- **Where to find:** ANI may recommend; also try Euro Freelancers, ResearchGate, LinkedIn

### Step 5: Submit NEB Boost (Month 3–4)
- **Trigger:** Prototype complete + 3 months sensor data
- **Amount:** €30,000
- **Use:** Covers monitoring extension, documentation, first workshop

### Step 6: Submit HORIZON-NEB-2026-01-REGEN-01 (Month 5–8)
- **Trigger:** Consortium secured (UAlg + LNEC + municipality + grant pro)
- **Deadline:** 01 December 2026
- **Amount:** Up to €4M
- **Use:** Scale prototype to 3–5 pilot homes, develop digital build manual, train-the-trainer programme

## Widening Participation — Special Opportunity

Portugal's **Widening** status gives Hestia access to instruments NOT available to higher-performing EU states:

| Instrument | Amount | Purpose | Hestia Fit |
|------------|--------|---------|------------|
| **Twinning** | Up to €1.5M | Link with leading European research org | Link with TU Delft / ETH Zurich / etc. for housing research |
| **Teaming for Excellence** | Co-funding | Create/upgrade centre of excellence | If we partner with UAlg to create "Centre for Affordable Housing" |
| **ERA Chairs** | €2.5M | Attract top international researcher | Hire renowned architect/engineer as figurehead |

**Contact:** FCT (fct.pt) for Widening-specific advisory
