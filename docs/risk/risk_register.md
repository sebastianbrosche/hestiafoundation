## Risk Assessment Methodology

**Likelihood Scale:**
- Very Low (1): <10% chance of occurring
- Low (2): 10–30% chance
- Medium (3): 30–60% chance
- High (4): 60–80% chance
- Very High (5): >80% chance

**Impact Scale:**
- Very Low (1): Minor delay or cost increase (<€1,000)
- Low (2): Moderate delay or cost increase (€1,000–€5,000)
- Medium (3): Significant delay or cost increase (€5,000–€15,000)
- High (4): Major delay or cost increase (€15,000–€50,000)
- Very High (5): Project failure or existential threat (>€50,000)

**Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact**
- **Critical (15–25):** Immediate action required, contingency plan mandatory
- **High (10–14):** Action required within 2 weeks, monitoring essential
- **Medium (6–9):** Action required within 1 month, regular monitoring
- **Low (1–5):** Accept and monitor, no immediate action

## Critical Risks (Score 15–25)

### R003: Grant Application Rejected (Score: 16)
**Impact:** Cannot fund research phase; project stalls or scales down significantly.
**Mitigation:**
1. Submit to minimum 3 programmes simultaneously
2. Hire grant professional with >80% success rate on Horizon/NEB
3. Build consortium with 3+ partners from 3 EU countries
4. Develop self-funding Plan B (workshop revenue, private donors)
5. Pre-write standard sections (capacity, track record, methodology)
6. Request pre-submission feedback from Commission

### R005: Cannot Secure Build Site (Score: 15)
**Impact:** Prototype cannot be built; all downstream activities blocked.
**Mitigation:**
1. Contact 3+ landowners in Lagos area simultaneously
2. Offer: publicity, research access, future workshop revenue share
3. Engage Lagos Câmara for municipal land or facilitation
4. Identify backup sites in neighbouring municipalities (Portimão, Lagoa)
5. Consider temporary site (12-month lease) if purchase/long-term unavailable
6. Budget for site rental if necessary

## Risk Monitoring and Reporting

### Monthly Risk Review
- Review all active risks
- Update likelihood/impact scores based on new information
- Verify mitigation actions are being executed
- Escalate any risk that has increased score

### Risk Escalation Trigger
Any risk that:
- Increases score by ≥3 points
- Becomes Critical (score ≥15)
- Has mitigation action overdue by ≥2 weeks

Must be reported to project steering committee within 48 hours.

### Risk Register Maintenance
- **Owner:** Project lead
- **Review frequency:** Monthly (or on demand)
- **Distribution:** All team leads, steering committee
- **Location:** `/workspace/risk_register.md` (this document)
