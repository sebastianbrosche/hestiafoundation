## 1. For Universities (Research Partnership)

### What's In It For You
- **First-mover research opportunity** — Zero peer-reviewed literature on pallet-based construction. Your faculty could publish the foundational papers.
- **Student theses** — Real-world structural engineering, thermal performance, and LCA projects. MSc and PhD opportunities.
- **Grant consortium** — Join our HORIZON-NEB-2026-01-REGEN-01 application (€4M). University partners strengthen our bid and get funded work packages.
- **Public visibility** — Open-source project with media attention. Your university's name on every publication.
- **Field lab** — A permanent test site in Algarve for ongoing research.

### What We Need From You
- Faculty advisor to supervise student research
- Access to testing equipment (if available) or joint funding for LNEC testing
- Letter of support for grant applications
- Optional: 1–2 students for 3–6 month research projects

### What We Offer
- Co-authorship on all publications
- Named partner in all media and grant materials
- Access to 12-month sensor dataset
- Invitation to build workshop (hands-on experience)
- Potential spin-off research projects beyond Hestia

## 2. For Municipalities (Urban Planning / Housing)

### What's In It For You
- **Affordable housing solution** for your constituents. A €25,000 home vs. €150,000+ conventional.
- **EU funding alignment** — NEB, Horizon Europe, LIFE Programme. We bring the project; you bring the land and community.
- **Circular economy showcase** — Reusing waste pallets, no concrete, low carbon. A visible sustainability project.
- **Community engagement** — 10-day build workshops create local employment skills and community cohesion.
- **Temporary / emergency housing** — Rapid deployment for homeless, refugees, or disaster response.

### What We Need From You
- Land access (200 m² minimum, flat, with vehicle access)
- Simplified permitting or regulatory guidance
- Introduction to local community and potential residents
- Letter of support for grant applications

### What We Offer
- First pilot home in your municipality (free or low-cost)
- Open-source documentation for your housing strategy
- Media coverage highlighting your progressive approach
- Training programme for local builders and officials
- Annual report on prototype performance for your planning records

## 3. For NGOs (Housing / Sustainability / Social Justice)

### What's In It For You
- **Proven system** — Not theoretical. A buildable, documented, tested system.
- **Open-source** — No licensing fees, no vendor lock-in. Your organisation can use, modify, and distribute freely.
- **Scalable** — From one prototype to hundreds of homes. The manual works for any team with basic tools.
- **Local materials** — Pallets are available everywhere. No import dependency.
- **Community-led** — Designed for self-build by residents, not dependency on contractors.

### What We Need From You
- Field testing in your region (adaptation to different climates)
- Community connections for pilot builds
- Translation of build manual into local languages
- Advocacy and dissemination in your network

### What We Offer
- Co-branded build manual and documentation
- Joint grant applications (your network + our system)
- Training for your field staff
- Priority access to design variations for your climate
- Public acknowledgment as founding partner

## 4. For Structural Engineers

### What's In It For You
- **Novel structural system** — First to analyse and sign off on pallet-based construction. Unique CV entry.
- **Publication opportunity** — Co-author on peer-reviewed structural engineering papers.
- **Eurocode application** — Apply EC 1 (loads), EC 5 (timber), EC 8 (seismic) to an unconventional system.
- **Low liability** — Small-scale prototype, not a high-rise. Manageable risk.
- **Public visibility** — Your name on an open-source project with growing attention.

### What We Need From You
- Structural calculation review (wind, seismic, live loads)
- Panel assembly analysis (shear, compression, racking)
- Foundation design for lightweight structure
- Signed documentation for permit application
- 2–3 hours/month advisory during build phase

### What We Offer
- €2,000–€6,000 fee for initial review
- Co-authorship on publications
- Public recognition on website and GitHub
- Potential for ongoing advisory role as project scales
- First-hand experience with an innovative system

## 5. For Builders / Contractors

### What's In It For You
- **New revenue stream** — Offer pallet-based construction as a low-cost alternative.
- **Marketing differentiation** — "We build €25,000 homes" is a powerful pitch.
- **Low material costs** — Pallets at €8–12 vs. timber framing at €40–60/m².
- **Speed** — 10-day build = faster turnover, more projects per year.
- **Open-source support** — Full manual, community support, continuous improvements.

### What We Need From You
- Build the prototype with us (volunteer or reduced fee)
- Feedback on build manual — what's confusing, what needs more detail
- Real-world cost data from your build
- Optional: Offer Hestia builds as a service in your region

### What We Offer
- Early access to complete build system
- Your company listed as "approved builder" on website
- Training and certification in Hestia build method
- Referrals from our website (once we have demand)
- Community of practice with other Hestia builders
